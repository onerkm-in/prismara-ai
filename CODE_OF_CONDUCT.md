# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Prismara AI to be a useful, respectful project for people building local-first and privacy-conscious AI tools. We welcome contributors with different backgrounds, skill levels, and viewpoints.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction gracefully.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and the work.
- Give credit for ideas and contributions.
- Help make the project easier for new contributors to understand.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic disruption.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors outside the project channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a concern, open a private communication channel with the project owner instead of posting sensitive details publicly.
